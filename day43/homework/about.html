About Delivered Fresh

Why we started Delivered Fresh

“There has to be a better way”. That is what we kept thinking. We loved shopping at our local farm markets, but it was so hard to get everything we needed. Sometimes the farmer wouldn’t be there, or they’d already be sold out of what we wanted. Or it would rain, and who wants to shop in the rain. If you struck out at one market, it would be a long drive to go to the next one. We kept thinking that it would be better for the farmer and for the consumer if they could just list what they had to offer and let us put in an order for what we wanted. It’d be even better if we could get it delivered. And that is exactly what Delivered Fresh does!

Delivered Fresh • Fresh Food Direct from Local Farms

Delivering pastured meat, organic produce, eggs, dairy, and pantry items to homes in Northeast Pennsylvania. We’re a local food hub, connecting you to our network of over 50 farms and producers, and helping the NE Pennsylvania farming community thrive.
