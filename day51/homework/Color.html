Mallorca
In Color



Mallorca is a divided island, having an exceptional ecological social microcosm. A mix of old people, tourists, locals and artists. The whole thing has an atmosphere that is difficult to explain, except perhaps through photography. I have the chance during the summer of 2022 to capture moments of life under the warmth of the Mediterranean sun. Take advantage!
